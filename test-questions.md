# Test Questions

Send these in Slack one at a time. Each group should cluster together and trigger an FAQ draft after the 3rd question.

---

## Group 1: VPN Access

1. How do I connect to the company VPN?
2. What are the steps to set up VPN access?
3. I can't figure out how to get on the VPN from home
4. Where do I find the VPN configuration settings?

## Group 2: PTO Requests

1. How do I submit a PTO request?
2. Where do I go to request time off?
3. What's the process for booking vacation days?
4. How do I put in for paid time off?

## Group 3: Expense Reports

1. How do I submit an expense report?
2. What's the process for getting reimbursed for expenses?
3. Where do I file my receipts for reimbursement?
4. How do I claim expenses from a work trip?
