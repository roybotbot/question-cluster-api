# Company Context (for FAQ drafting)

Use this as a reference when populating the LLM Chain prompt in n8n.

## Systems & Tools
- **VPN:** Cisco AnyConnect. Download from internal IT portal at it.company.com/vpn. Requires MFA through Okta.
- **PTO/Time Off:** Requested through BambooHR. Go to Time Off > Request Time Off. Manager approval required. Submit at least 2 weeks in advance.
- **Expense Reports:** Filed through Expensify. Receipts required for anything over $25. Submit within 30 days of expense. Reimbursement takes 5-7 business days.
- **Password Reset:** Self-service through Okta at company.okta.com. Click "Forgot Password" on the login page. IT can also reset manually via Slack #it-help.
- **Printers:** Managed by PaperCut. Add printers from System Preferences > Printers using the server address print.internal.company.com. For jams, power cycle the printer and clear trays. If persistent, submit a ticket in #facilities.
- **Communication:** Slack for internal chat, Google Meet for video calls, Gmail for email.
- **Project Management:** Jira for engineering, Asana for non-engineering teams.
- **Documentation:** Notion for internal wikis and docs.
- **Code:** GitHub Enterprise. Access requests go through #it-help.
- **SSO:** All apps use Okta SSO. New accounts provisioned by IT within 24 hours of start date.

## Common Policies
- Remote work: Hybrid, 3 days in office (Tue/Wed/Thu).
- Equipment: Laptops issued by IT on first day. Replacement requests through #it-help.
- Onboarding: First-week checklist in Notion under People Ops > Onboarding.
